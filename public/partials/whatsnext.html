WHAT'S NEXT

Let's share ideas on what to do next with this technology.

Live Translation.

We want to use the power of speech to text on smart glasses combined with the power of computer-generated translation to power a live translation tool in the near future.

With the programs we already have, pluging a live-translation module is not too hard. The challenges are to keep our processing speed the same, and being able to detect, change and pick-up any language around - no matter your accent or weird expressions.

This tool in our hand - and on your eyes - not only would you be able to read every conversation, but understand it as well. No need to take on norwegian 6 months before your trip to Oslo anymore!
